About WiggMap

WiggMap helps you compare countries before moving abroad — cost of living, salaries, rent, taxes, healthcare, quality of life, and more.

🚧 Work in progress

WiggMap is a newly launched project and is still under active development. Most figures are indicative and may evolve over time.

The strength of WiggMap comes from its community. If you notice outdated or incorrect data, you can use the “Correct data” button (or the ✏️ icons on country pages) to suggest updates. Contributions are truly appreciated — they help make the platform more accurate and useful for everyone.
