Safety is a meaningful differentiator here. Panama scores 40 / 100 on the crime index vs 55 / 100 for Mexico — a gap that shapes daily life, insurance costs and the mental load of living abroad.

Panama is for retirees and remote workers seeking a dollarised economy, tropical climate, tax-friendly residency and easy access to both oceans. Mexico is for digital nomads, retirees and sun-seekers drawn to rich culture, proximity to the US, accessible residency and highly affordable living in cities like Oaxaca and Mérida.
